Account & Membership

Last updated: 2025-10-27

Our membership program rewards loyal customers with enhanced support, faster service, and extended return windows. Each tier automatically applies to qualifying purchases and renews annually based on your total spend or purchase frequency.

Tier Benefits

Tier Benefit Return Window
Standard Entry-level membership automatically applied to all registered customers. Includes access to order tracking, standard shipping options, and email support during normal business hours. 14 days
Silver Elite Enjoy standard customer support with access to our self-service Knowledge Base and email assistance. Members also receive early access to seasonal sales and new product releases. 30 days
Gold Medallion Gain priority access to phone and chat support with reduced wait times. Members benefit from complimentary two-day shipping on all domestic orders and 10% off accessories year-round. 45 days
Platinum Premium Receive exclusive 24/7 access to our dedicated support line with direct escalation to senior specialists. Members enjoy free express shipping, extended warranties on eligible electronics, and invitations to limited product trials and launch events. 60 days

How to Upgrade: Log in to your account dashboard and view your membership progress tracker. Tiers adjust automatically based on your annual purchase volume. You’ll receive an email notification when you move up to the next level.
