Warranty FAQs

Last updated: 2025-10-27

Our limited warranty covers defects in materials or workmanship that occur under normal consumer use. Proof of purchase and the product serial number may be required for verification. Damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications is not covered.

Eligibility & Claim Steps

Electronics

  • Coverage: 12 months
  • Includes products such as smart kettles, speakers, and connected devices.
  • A valid serial number must be provided for verification.
  • Warranty covers manufacturing defects and internal component failures only.
  • Accidental damage or liquid exposure is excluded.

Accessories

  • Coverage: 6 months
  • Covers chargers, cables, and wearable accessories.
  • A dated sales receipt or online order confirmation is required.
  • Cosmetic wear such as scratches, discoloration, or normal aging is not covered.

How to File a Claim

  • Contact our support team through your My Account portal or email support@innovateretail.demo.
  • Provide proof of purchase, product serial number, and a brief description of the issue.
  • A representative will review your claim within two business days and provide next steps for repair or replacement.
